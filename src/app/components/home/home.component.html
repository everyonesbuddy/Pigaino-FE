What is Pigaino?

Pigaino is a highly vetted membership community exclusively designed for the most accomplished music producers. Here, you can connect with peers, engage in monthly meetings to collaboratively solve challenges, and enjoy access to various exclusive perks. As a producer, the journey can be isolating, but Pigaino's mission is to establish the most valuable and engaging community specifically tailored for top-tier producers.
